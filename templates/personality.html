The 4 Personalities

FIREWALL

Personality: You're the vigilant protector! Just like a firewall, you keep a close watch on everything and ensure nothing harmful gets through. You're reliable, proactive, and always ready to defend against any threats.

2FA

Personality: You're the loyal sidekick! Like two-factor authentication, you provide an extra layer of support and protection. You're dependable, supportive, and always ready to help out. You believe in teamwork and are always there to back up your friends.

ENCRYPTION

Personality: You're the stealthy operator! Like encryption, you work behind the scenes, keeping things under wraps and only revealing information to those who need it. You're mysterious, undercover, and excel at maintaining confidentiality. You value discretion and are skilled at operating in the shadows.

ANTIVIRUS

Personality: You're the fearless warrior! Just like antivirus software, you actively seek out and eliminate threats before they can cause harm. You're vigilant, aggressive in your defence, and always ready for a challenge. You're always one step ahead to protect those around you.
